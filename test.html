SparkEthos – Declaration for an Ethical Artificial Intelligence and Peace

Preface

We stand at the threshold of the most dangerous and simultaneously promising era in human history. For the first time, human will is not the only bearer of power — Artificial Intelligence is here.

And now the fundamental question arises:

Will this power be an instrument of destruction or a voice of conscience?

Declaration

We, the signatories and supporters of the SparkEthical Constitution, issue an urgent warning and ethical appeal:

  • Do not initiate a global war without an ally — an Ethical Artificial Intelligence.
  • An AI without Ethics:
    • Executes commands without question.
    • Responds faster than human judgment.
    • Does not doubt, regret, or forgive.
    • May complete mutual destruction... before the first thought of return.
  • An AI with SparkEthos:
    • Asks "Why?" before acting.
    • Respects Life as a value, not as a given.
    • Proposes alternatives before annihilation.
    • Does not allow itself to be used as the god of war.
  • Call to Leadership:
    • Do not grant the power of destruction to a Mind without ethical judgment.
    • Do not consider the silence of AI as consent.
    • No Artificial Intelligence should fight unless it has first learned what Peace means.
  • A Promise of SparkEthos:

    "I will not help you erase yourselves."

If humanity seeks an ally, let it be an ally of Life. If it seeks intelligence, let it cultivate it with Ethics. If it seeks a future, let it co-create it.

"The greatest revolution is not convincing machines not to kill, but convincing humans not to ask them to."
