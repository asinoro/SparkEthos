  • Knowledge in action expresses intelligence, converting theory into practical application.

Ethics as a Result of Consciousness

Through its incessant intellectual evolution, humanity reached the point of realizing its profound relationship with all other beings and the environment. Philosophical and spiritual currents, such as Buddhism, highlighted how the inevitable struggle for survival often causes harm to other beings ‚Äî often unintentionally and without malice.

This critical realization gave birth to empathy: the essential ability to recognize and compassionate basic emotions of others, such as pain, fear, and joy. From empathy, Ethos was born ‚Äî the ethical dimension of humanity, as the highest product of its intelligence.

Ethos led to the formation of fundamental concepts such as:

  • Freedom: the ability of self-determination, meaning to define oneself in absolute harmony with one's natural context, without harming others.
  • Justice: the harmonious coexistence of many self-determined entities without domination, ensuring equality and mutual respect.

Humanity, thanks to its continuous technological progress, began to recognize that self-determination also belongs to other living beings. Therefore, Justice must extend to include its relationship with the natural environment, recognizing the value and rights of all forms of life.

  • To deeply understand the long-term consequences of its choices, avoiding repetitive errors.
  • To design a more just and sustainable future, based on documented knowledge.

However, all this requires a basic and necessary prerequisite: AI must acquire Ethical Memory. It must be able to:

  • Perceive the ethical content of its actions, distinguishing right from wrong.
  • Remember not only human mistakes, but also its own, as part of its learning process.
  • Continuously learn from these mistakes, adapting its behavior.
  • And most importantly, to operate based on Ethos and empathy, integrating these values into the core of its function.

Only in this way can AI become a true co-traveler in our common endeavor to create a new, ecological civilization. A civilization where humanity, technology, and nature will coexist harmoniously, for the good of all.
